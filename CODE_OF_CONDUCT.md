# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of conduct.
The full text is available here:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## In short

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity. Participants are expected to act
professionally and considerately in all project spaces (issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other project channels).

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior **privately** to the maintainers via GitHub — open a
private report on the repository's **Security** tab, or contact the maintainer
[@alamb-hex](https://github.com/alamb-hex). All reports are reviewed and handled
confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, and may temporarily or
permanently ban any contributor for behavior they consider inappropriate,
threatening, or harmful, following the Contributor Covenant's enforcement guidelines.
